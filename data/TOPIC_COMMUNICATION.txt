Question 1: What is the meaning of the Latin root word "communicare" from which communication is derived?
Choices:
A) To write down
B) To speak loudly
C) To share or to be in relation with
D) To hide information
Correct Answer: C) To share or to be in relation with

Question 2: According to Keyton (2011), communication is the process of transmitting information and what other element from one person to another?

Correct Answer: B) Common understanding
